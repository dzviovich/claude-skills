## Useful Search Strings

- `"best laptop for [use case] [year]" site:wirecutter.com`
- `"[model] review" site:notebookcheck.net`
- `"MacBook Air vs MacBook Pro [year]"`
- `"best value laptop under $[budget] [year]"`
- eBay: `[model name] -parts -repair condition:"Used - Good" OR "Certified Refurbished"`
