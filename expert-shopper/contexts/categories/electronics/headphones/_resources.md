## Key Search Strings

- `"best noise canceling headphones [year]" site:rtings.com`
- `"[model] review" site:rtings.com` — for objective measurements
- `"[model] call quality" OR "microphone quality"`
- `"best headphones for [use case] under $[budget] [year]"`
- eBay: `[model] headphones -parts -repair condition:"Used - Good"`

## RTINGS.com Tips

RTINGS is the gold standard for objective headphone measurements. Key pages to check:
- Headphone comparison tool: https://www.rtings.com/headphones/tools/compare
- Best ANC headphones ranking: https://www.rtings.com/headphone/1-0-0/graph#620
- Use their "Test Bench" score breakdowns for: ANC, microphone, comfort, sound
