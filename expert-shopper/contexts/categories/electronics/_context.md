# Category Context: Electronics

Parent category for: `laptops/`, `headphones/`, `smartphones/`, `tvs/`, `cameras/`, etc.

## General Electronics Buying Principles

- **Compatibility is king**: Always ask about existing ecosystem (Apple, Android, Windows, etc.)
  before recommending anything. A Sony headphone might be great but irrelevant if they're
  deep in the Apple ecosystem.
- **Refurb is generally safe**: Electronics from reputable refurb programs (Apple Certified,
  manufacturer-direct, Back Market Grade A) are excellent value. The risk is lower than
  most categories.
- **Check the release cycle**: Before recommending a product, check if a new version is
  imminent. Recommending a product that gets refreshed in 2 months is unhelpful.
- **Warranty matters**: For high-ticket electronics, ask if they want extended warranty
  coverage. Costco and Amex provide notable extensions.
- **Region/voltage**: Most consumer electronics auto-switch 100–240V. Confirm for higher-end
  or older specialty equipment.
