## Key Search Strings

- `"best espresso machine [year]" site:nytimes.com/wirecutter`
- `"[model] review" site:home-barista.com`
- `"espresso machine under $[budget] [year]" reddit`
- `"[model] common problems" OR "known issues"`
- eBay: `"[model name]" espresso -portafilter -parts condition:"Used - Good"`

## Important Notes

- Many espresso machines are region-specific (voltage 110V vs 220V). Always confirm voltage
  for the user's country.
- European brands (De'Longhi, Gaggia, Lelit, ECM, Rocket) often have excellent Italian build
  quality; US support/warranty service varies — check before recommending.
